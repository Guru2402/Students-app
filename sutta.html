Welcome To Chandrakanthi Public School

Chandrakanthi public school located at Peelamedu, Coimbatore is one of the best CBSE schools which follows Montessori curriculum in nursery levels and Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) curriculum from grade I.

Schools shoulder an important responsibility of ensuring holistic development of children. Armed with the 50 year lineage of the GRG Trust, Chandrakanthi Public School, Affiliated to CBSE, is committed to deliver ‘best-in-class’ education to its wards across the social and economic spectrum of Coimbatore.

The guiding light behind all these institutions is the visionary zeal and love for children of late Smt. Chandrakanthi Govindarajulu, which continues to inspire Chandrakanthi Public School in its purported objective.

Dr. R. Nandini

Message from Correspondent

At the outset, let me welcome you and your child to Chandrakanthi Public School. As you know, this is a very special time in your child's life. He/ She will be learning many new skills - academic, social, emotional and motor skills. Parents play as important a role as the schools in helping their children reach their maximum potential. Research points out that most of a child's intellectual development takes place before he or she enters a formal classroom. This means that children spend a great deal of time with their first teachers, their parents. Indeed, as parents, your involvement in your child's learning experiences will have much to do with how well your child succeeds in school and later in life.
